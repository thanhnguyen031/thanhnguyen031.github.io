What is My Hobby?

My hobby is traveling with my family in the summer break.

Who am I?

My name is Thanh Nguyen (Binny), and I'm a senior in Computer Engineering major at UNCC. My best hobby is traveling around the world with my family during my free time.

When is the Best Time?

The best time to enjoy traveling is during the summer because I have a three-month break from school. This allows me to explore different places around the world and experience things I have never seen before. Holidays like Christmas and New Year are also great times to travel, as many destinations offer festive celebrations.

Where to Enjoy?

I usually travel to Asian countries, mainly visiting Vietnam since it is my hometown. After that, my family and I explore other countries like Thailand, Singapore, Malaysia, and various other destinations in Asia.

How to Get Started?

Save as much money as you can so you can treat yourself to a trip to a place you've always wanted to visit. Sometimes, you only live once, and it's important to do what you love.

Why Do This Hobby?

Traveling helps people escape the stress of school and work. It also allows individuals to experience the beauty of the world and learn about different cultures from various countries.
